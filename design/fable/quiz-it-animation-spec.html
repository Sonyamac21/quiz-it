TRANSITIONS & ENTRANCES

THE BREATH — screen/page transition
dim 160ms → hold ≥120ms dark → enter 240ms settle
All state-to-state changes on all three surfaces. Darken → place → light. Never a crossfade of content.
Answers are in.
RISE & SETTLE — standard entrance
240ms · ease-settle · translateY 16→0
Default for content. Lists stagger 40ms/item, max 6 staggered.
TIME'S UP
THE STAMP — ceremonial entrance
400ms · ease-settle · scale 1.15→1 + 120ms light-shock
Countdown digits, END OF ROUND, CHAMPIONS, captions. Stamps only — never regular content.
EXIT
160ms · ease-exit · fade + scale .94
Exits never block entrances and are always faster than arrivals.

PLAY — COUNTDOWN, LOCK, REVEAL

TIMER RING — drain & final five
linear drain · final-5: stroke 4→10px, 12px contraction step/sec, white
The only sustained animation in play. Never red, never yellow. Phones Pulse in sync.

SCORE COUNT-UP
400–900ms by delta · ease-land · glow-pulse on landing
Rolling tabular digits. Deductions: 240ms drop, no count-down, brief red edge.
LOCKED
ANSWER LOCK — the seal
400ms · ease-land · edge-light trace + Lock haptic (firm double-knock)
Purple, never green — locked ≠ correct. The product's signature physical moment.
THE CRACK — correct reveal
ignite 300ms ease-land + collapse 160ms ease-exit, simultaneous
A switch, not a sequence. One survivor in green light; wrong options to 25%. Handset verdict lands a half-beat later.
INCORRECT — the thud
400ms · ease-land · red edge <1s, 3px settle-down, then 50%
Weight down, quickly done. No shake, no flash-train, no sad effects.

SHOW MOMENTS

LEADERBOARD TRAVEL — the overtake
700ms (+100ms/3 ranks, cap 1200) · ease-settle · trail decays 400ms
Mover lifts (scale 1.02 + float shadow), travels above resting rows; displaced rows shuffle at 60% speed. Max two movers, sequenced. Falls are never animated downward.
POWER CARD LAUNCH
400ms · ease-exit · upward + light trail; fan-in 240ms stagger
Hold-to-play charge precedes (linear fill + rising Pulse). Display announces the play simultaneously.
HARD DECK CARD FLIP
300ms · single rotateX pass · heavy strike on landing (room-wide haptic)
One flip, no multi-spin. Pot counts up after; ladder rung ignites.
PURSUIT RUNNER — advance
600ms travel + 100ms settle · ease-settle · trail + 3 sparks · sprint 900ms doubled-trail
All survivors animate simultaneously — one synchronized surge. Compaction reflow (700ms) only after the pass settles.
PURSUIT RUNNER — stumble & fall
stumble 150ms → slide 400ms ease-land → seated · lane desat 500ms
Ends seated facing the race, banked tally bright. Deliberately slower than success: weight, not comedy.
HOLD-TO-COMMIT CHARGE
linear fill (honesty) · tremble ±0.6px · mirrored on Display for Stick/Gamble & Spin
The room watches hesitation as light. Release before full = decay back at 2× speed.

FINALE & SYSTEM

CHAMPIONS
GOLD FLOOD — coronation
2s ease-land to sustained state · once per night
The venue changes colour. Held perfectly still during photos — nothing pulses while cameras are out.
CONFETTI — storm → drift → gone
burst ≤2s · gravity decay ~6s · full clear ≤10s · gold+white only
Coronation only. Varied tumble per piece. Never over text. No infinite loops.
LIGHT RAYS
conic gold rays · 6–14s slow rotate · ≤9% opacity
Coronation backdrop only. Slow enough to read as light, never as motion.
