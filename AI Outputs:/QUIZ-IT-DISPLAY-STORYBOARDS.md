# QUIZ-IT DISPLAY STORYBOARDS

**Cinematic frame-by-frame blueprints for the ten moments**
Quiz-It · Powered by Mac Entertainment · by Sonya Mac
Version 1.0 · July 2026 · Implements the Live Show Spec v1.0 using Design System v1.0 tokens

**Reading these boards:** Each moment is five frames. "Lighting" describes both on-screen light and the light the screen throws into the room (Live Show A1). "Timing" gives target durations; any frame marked **[HOST HOLD]** lasts as long as the host chooses — the trigger, not the clock, advances the show. Handset behaviour is noted where it is part of the picture.

---

## 1. OPENING TITLES — "The Ignition"

**Frame 1 — Blackout**
- **Audience sees:** The screen — whatever it showed — drops to `stage/deep`, effectively black. Nothing on it.
- **Motion:** Single fast fade-out, then absolute stillness.
- **Lighting:** Light leaves the room. Faces dim. Heads turn toward the dark screen.
- **Audio:** Silence. Venue noise becomes suddenly audible — that's the point.
- **Timing:** 3s of held black.
- **Transition:** Out of stillness, a pulse begins.
- **Emotional objective:** Interruption. "Something's starting."

**Frame 2 — The Heartbeat**
- **Audience sees:** A small purple bloom at centre, swelling and relaxing — a slow visual heartbeat.
- **Motion:** Bloom breathes, 1 beat/sec, growing slightly larger each beat.
- **Lighting:** Faint purple wash begins to touch nearby tables, brightening beat by beat.
- **Audio:** Deep sub-bass thump on each beat — felt, not loud. Every joined phone Pulses in sync.
- **Timing:** ~7s (7 beats).
- **Transition:** On the final beat the bloom snaps wide open.
- **Emotional objective:** Entrainment. The room's pulse locks to the show's.

**Frame 3 — The Roll-Call**
- **Audience sees:** Team crests sweep across the stage in procession — every team in the room, each catching an edge-light flare as it passes centre.
- **Motion:** Fast lateral procession, `ease/settle` on each centre-flare; crests exit accelerating (`ease/exit`).
- **Lighting:** Rhythmic flares strobe gently through the room with each crest.
- **Audio:** A rising ticking-shimmer bed; each team's phone gives a private Tick as their crest crosses.
- **Timing:** ~12s regardless of team count (procession speed scales).
- **Transition:** The procession curves inward, crests spiralling into the bloom.
- **Emotional objective:** Recognition. "That's US on the screen." Tables point.

**Frame 4 — Convergence and black**
- **Audience sees:** All crests collapse into a single point of purple light — which dies. One beat of total black.
- **Motion:** Fast inward spiral, `ease/exit`; then nothing.
- **Lighting:** Room drops dark again — deeper than before because eyes have adapted.
- **Audio:** The bed cuts dead. One beat of silence.
- **Timing:** 1.5s + 1s black.
- **Transition:** Detonation.
- **Emotional objective:** The intake of breath.

**Frame 5 — The Ignition**
- **Audience sees:** **QUIZ-IT** wordmark stamps on at full scale (Bruno Ace SC, "QUIZ-" `brand/purple`, "IT" white), light-shock flaring the frame; venue name lands beneath at `display/support`.
- **Motion:** Stamp entrance (§5.3), bloom exploding outward to full-bleed, settling to a confident glow.
- **Lighting:** The room floods purple — the night's first full-room colour moment.
- **Audio:** The full sonic logo, fortissimo. Then a quick duck to silence as the host's mic goes live.
- **Timing:** 3s to settle. **[HOST HOLD]** — the host walks into the light.
- **Transition:** Wordmark shrinks to its Whisper badge position as Round 1's stage sets.
- **Emotional objective:** Arrival. "This is a show."

## 2. QUESTION REVEAL — "The Deal"

**Frame 1 — The Dim**
- **Audience sees:** The ambient stage darkens one step; the resting standings and wordmark fade back.
- **Motion:** Global brightness ramp down, `motion/fast`. Stillness follows.
- **Lighting:** Room dims slightly — the learned "look up" cue.
- **Audio:** Ambient bed fades out. Quiet.
- **Timing:** 0.5s.
- **Transition:** The card arrives into the cleared dark.
- **Emotional objective:** Reflex attention. Conversation drops mid-sentence.

**Frame 2 — The Deal-In**
- **Audience sees:** A large card back snaps to centre — crest-embossed, edge-lit — carrying "QUESTION 7" and the category chip.
- **Motion:** Deals in from lower-off-screen with overshoot-settle (`ease/settle`, `motion/standard`).
- **Lighting:** Single bloom ignites behind the card. Rest of stage stays dark.
- **Audio:** One crisp dealt-card sting. Handsets: deal-in Pulse ("pick up your phone").
- **Timing:** 0.4s.
- **Transition:** Holds face-down for the host.
- **Emotional objective:** Readiness — a dealer has just slid you cards.

**Frame 3 — The Tease [HOST HOLD]**
- **Audience sees:** The card back, still, breathing minutely with the bloom. Category visible; question hidden.
- **Motion:** None but the bloom breath. Deliberate stillness.
- **Lighting:** Unchanged; the held bloom keeps all eyes on the card.
- **Audio:** Nothing from the system — the host's patter is the soundtrack.
- **Timing:** Entirely the host's. Two seconds or twenty.
- **Transition:** Host triggers the flip.
- **Emotional objective:** Loaded anticipation, owned by a human voice.

**Frame 4 — The Flip**
- **Audience sees:** The card flips once — fast, clean — and the question stands at `display/statement`, alone.
- **Motion:** Single 300ms rotateX pass; question text settles with a subtle rise.
- **Lighting:** Bloom brightens a step as the face lands.
- **Audio:** One clean flip note.
- **Timing:** 0.3s.
- **Transition:** Options arrive beneath.
- **Emotional objective:** Clarity — the whole room reads it in one look.

**Frame 5 — Play Opens**
- **Audience sees:** Answer options land top-to-bottom in a fast stagger; the timer ring ignites around the screen edge and begins to drain.
- **Motion:** 40ms stagger per option, `ease/settle`; ring fades in and starts its sweep.
- **Lighting:** Steady, bright enough to read faces by; the ring adds a live edge to the room.
- **Audio:** Low tension bed enters (ducks under host's voice). Handsets: live question mirrored, thumbs go to work.
- **Timing:** 0.6s to fully open; play runs 15–30s by question type.
- **Transition:** Into the Close (Moment 3) or straight to seal on early locks.
- **Emotional objective:** Focus. The social room becomes a competitive one.

## 3. FINAL FIVE SECONDS — "The Close"

**Frame 1 — The Cut**
- **Audience sees:** At T-5s the screen changes state: tension bed dies, stage darkens a step, options brighten by contrast, the ring thickens.
- **Motion:** One global step-change, `motion/fast`. Then stillness before the first thump.
- **Lighting:** Room dims — the pre-storm drop.
- **Audio:** Music cuts dead. Silence for half a beat.
- **Timing:** 0.4s.
- **Transition:** The first heartbeat lands.
- **Emotional objective:** Alarm without panic: "it's ending."

**Frame 2 — Five · Four**
- **Audience sees:** Giant numeral **5** stamps centre (`display/numeral-hero`), ring contracts one step inward; then **4**.
- **Motion:** Stamp per second (§5.3); ring steps are sharp, not smooth — the walls close in clicks.
- **Lighting:** Light-shock per stamp — the room flares once per second.
- **Audio:** The Quiz-It heartbeat: one deep dual-thump per second. Un-locked phones Pulse in sync.
- **Timing:** 2s.
- **Transition:** Continuous into 3-2-1.
- **Emotional objective:** Unison. Screen, sound, and sixty hands beating together.

**Frame 3 — Three · Two**
- **Audience sees:** **3**, **2** — numerals larger still; stage visibly darker; options and ring now the brightest things in the room.
- **Motion:** Same stamp rhythm; contraction steps deepen.
- **Lighting:** Each flare stronger against a darker base — contrast climbing.
- **Audio:** Heartbeat unchanged in tempo (never accelerates — dread lives in relentlessness), slightly louder.
- **Timing:** 2s.
- **Transition:** The last second.
- **Emotional objective:** Squeeze. Regulars are chanting the numbers.

**Frame 4 — One → Zero**
- **Audience sees:** **1** stamps — beat — then **TIME'S UP** slams across the stage as the ring snaps shut to a sealed circle.
- **Motion:** Final stamp, ring seal (fast inward collapse, `ease/exit`), then total stillness.
- **Lighting:** One last, biggest flare — then the stage settles dark and still.
- **Audio:** Heavy vault-shut note. Every phone strikes one final Hit. Then **dead silence**.
- **Timing:** 1.2s.
- **Transition:** Into the reveal silence (Moment 4, Frame 1).
- **Emotional objective:** The held breath. Silence louder than the thumps.

**Frame 5 — The Handover**
- **Audience sees:** Question and options standing sealed and still on the dark stage. Nothing moves.
- **Motion:** None. Stillness is the frame.
- **Lighting:** Low, steady; the sealed options faintly edge-lit.
- **Audio:** Nothing. The host's voice owns the room.
- **Timing:** **[HOST HOLD]**.
- **Transition:** The Crack.
- **Emotional objective:** Suspense transferred from machine to human.

*Escalation note: the final question of the night runs this board at Gear 3 — heartbeat deeper and slower, numerals at maximum, darkness closing further.*

## 4. ANSWER REVEAL — "The Crack"

**Frame 1 — The Silence [HOST HOLD]**
- **Audience sees:** The sealed stage from the Close: question, options, stillness.
- **Motion:** None.
- **Lighting:** Low and even. No hints — no option may glow early.
- **Audio:** None. Host performs into the quiet.
- **Timing:** Host's choice; the software will hold forever.
- **Transition:** Host trigger.
- **Emotional objective:** Maximum leaning-in.

**Frame 2 — The Crack**
- **Audience sees:** The correct option **ignites** `feature/correct` at full intensity; every wrong option collapses to near-black in the same instant. One survivor in light.
- **Motion:** Simultaneous: ignition (light-shock) + collapse (`motion/fast` dim). Nothing travels; it's a switch, not a sequence.
- **Lighting:** The room flashes green for one breath — the night's most repeated colour event.
- **Audio:** One percussive hit with a short green shimmer tail. Handsets: private verdicts (Hit+Cascade or Thud) land a half-beat after.
- **Timing:** 0.3s to full state.
- **Transition:** Hold one beat for the roar.
- **Emotional objective:** Release. Cheers and groans in the same instant.

**Frame 3 — The Survivor**
- **Audience sees:** The correct answer alone in green light, question dimmed above it — the fact of the matter, unmissable.
- **Motion:** Held state; green edge settles from flare to steady.
- **Lighting:** Green glow softens; room light returns toward neutral purple-dark.
- **Audio:** Quiet again — the host reacts with the room.
- **Timing:** 2s.
- **Transition:** The receipt slides in.
- **Emotional objective:** Confirmation — everyone knows, together.

**Frame 4 — The Receipt**
- **Audience sees:** One `display/support` line beneath: **"9 of 14 teams got it."**
- **Motion:** Rise-and-settle, `motion/standard`.
- **Lighting:** No change — this frame is information, not spectacle.
- **Audio:** A single soft tick. The line is the host's springboard ("FIVE teams missed it!").
- **Timing:** Lands 2.5s after the crack; holds through host patter.
- **Transition:** Host advances.
- **Emotional objective:** Communal comparison — the applause engine.

**Frame 5 — The Clear**
- **Audience sees:** Question, answer and receipt fall away; the stage exhales back to ambient (or into the next deal).
- **Motion:** Exit fade + scale-down, `motion/fast`; bloom returns to resting breath.
- **Lighting:** Room returns to its between-questions warmth.
- **Audio:** Ambient bed re-enters at low level.
- **Timing:** 0.5s.
- **Transition:** Moment 2 (next deal) or Moment 5 (standings).
- **Emotional objective:** Reset. Ready for the next hand.

## 5. LEADERBOARD — "The Overtake"

**Frame 1 — The Stamp**
- **Audience sees:** The stage clears to dark; **STANDINGS** stamps across it.
- **Motion:** Stamp entrance with light-shock; then the word slides to the stage's upper edge as a header.
- **Lighting:** One flare, then a cool, even broadcast light.
- **Audio:** A short propulsive sting kicks the build bed in. Handsets drop to Eyes Up.
- **Timing:** 1s.
- **Transition:** Rows begin building immediately — no dead air.
- **Emotional objective:** "Here comes the story."

**Frame 2 — The Fast Build (bottom half)**
- **Audience sees:** Rows land bottom-up in quick rhythm — crest, name, score — lower places arriving with full polish and zero dwell.
- **Motion:** Row entrances at 3–4 rows/sec, rise-and-settle; scores roll to values as they land.
- **Lighting:** Steady; each row lands with a faint tick of edge light.
- **Audio:** Rhythmic build bed, tick per row. Each team's handset strikes its private Hit as their row lands — a wave of buzzes climbing the room.
- **Timing:** ~4s for the bottom half.
- **Transition:** The build audibly and visibly slows.
- **Emotional objective:** Kind speed — everyone's on the board, nobody's shamed.

**Frame 3 — The Overtake**
- **Audience sees:** The biggest mover **travels**: a crest climbs past resting rows with a purple glow-trail, caption chip stamping on: **"▲ THE QUIZZLY BEARS — up 3."**
- **Motion:** Vertical travel, `motion/showpiece`, `ease/settle`; displaced rows shuffle down softly; max two movers per board.
- **Lighting:** The moving row carries its own light through the field.
- **Audio:** A rising whoosh-and-lock; the mover's handsets Cascade.
- **Timing:** 1.2s per overtake.
- **Transition:** Build resumes into the top five, one row per beat.
- **Emotional objective:** The race made visible — a mid-table team hears their name roared.

**Frame 4 — The Hold Below Three**
- **Audience sees:** Rows 5 and 4 land… and the build **stops**. Three empty slots glow faintly at the top. Nothing moves.
- **Motion:** Dead stop. The empty top three breathe with the bloom.
- **Lighting:** Stage dims a step; the three vacant slots are the brightest objects.
- **Audio:** Bed drops to a single held pulse. **[HOST HOLD]** — "so who's on the podium places…"
- **Timing:** Host's choice.
- **Transition:** Host trigger releases the top three.
- **Emotional objective:** The learned reflex: when the build pauses, cheer.

**Frame 5 — Third, Second… First**
- **Audience sees:** Row 3 lands. Beat. Row 2 lands. Longer beat. Row 1 lands with a purple edge-flare and a score roll.
- **Motion:** One row per host beat, each with a slightly larger settle; first place's numerals roll last.
- **Lighting:** Rising steps of brightness; first place's landing throws the biggest flare of the sequence.
- **Audio:** Rising note per row; one clean hit on first. Then out — total board time under a minute.
- **Timing:** 4–8s, host-paced.
- **Transition:** Board rests briefly, then clears to ambient or next deal.
- **Emotional objective:** Status confirmed, drama banked, pace intact.

## 6. SPIN TO WIN — "The Machine"

**Frame 1 — The Summons**
- **Audience sees:** Stage floods deep purple; **SPIN TO WIN** ignites in Bruno Ace SC; beneath it stamps the earning: **"Fastest correct answer: [crest] THE QUIZZLY BEARS."**
- **Motion:** Title stamp + light-shock; the crest and name rise beneath with settle.
- **Lighting:** Full purple flood — the room changes colour; the glam moment announces itself.
- **Audio:** The Spin to Win signature — bright, metallic, arena-glam. All other handsets drop to Eyes Up ("[Team] is at the wheel"); the chosen team's phones ignite.
- **Timing:** ~5s.
- **Transition:** The title compresses to a header; the machine builds.
- **Emotional objective:** Spotlight, publicly earned — applause point one.

**Frame 2 — The Machine Builds**
- **Audience sees:** A colossal jewel-lit slot machine assembles on stage — chassis, lights, three shut reels — physical, glinting, expensive.
- **Motion:** Assembly in bold snapping pieces (`ease/settle`, staggered), not a fade-in. It should feel *installed*.
- **Lighting:** The machine's lamps flicker on row by row, throwing moving glints across the room.
- **Audio:** Mechanical rack-and-latch sounds under a shimmer bed. **[HOST HOLD]** — "who's pressing?"
- **Timing:** 2s build, then host-held.
- **Transition:** The team touches their SPIN control.
- **Emotional objective:** Object desire — the room wants to see it run.

**Frame 3 — The Charge**
- **Audience sees:** As the chosen team holds SPIN on their phone, the machine **responds live**: reels trembling, light climbing the chassis, the whole machine straining.
- **Motion:** Trembling amplitude and light height map to the held charge (`ease/charge` honesty).
- **Lighting:** Climbing brightness — the room senses the crescendo without being told.
- **Audio:** A rising mechanical whine; the team's phones Pulse rising in hand.
- **Timing:** As long as the team dares hold (natural cap ~3s).
- **Transition:** Release.
- **Emotional objective:** One thumb, sixty witnesses — the controller doctrine as theatre.

**Frame 4 — The Spin, Reel by Reel**
- **Audience sees:** Reels explode into blur. Then: reel one lands — *chunk*. Reel two — *chunk* — the room does the maths, noise rising. A held beat… 
- **Motion:** Full-speed blur to sequenced left-to-right landings; each landing has weight and a micro-settle.
- **Lighting:** Strobing reel-light during the blur; a focused snap of light on each landed reel.
- **Audio:** Clatter under the blur; a heavy mechanical *chunk* per landing — **every phone in the room strikes with each one.** Half-beat of silence before reel three.
- **Timing:** 5–8s total; the pause before the last reel is the longest.
- **Transition:** The third reel decides the frame that follows.
- **Emotional objective:** Communal suspense, honestly staged — no fake near-misses, ever.

**Frame 5 — The Verdict**
- **Audience sees:** **Win:** the machine erupts — light cascade, prize stamps at `display/statement`, team name in lights. **Miss:** the machine dims gracefully; caption: "So close, [team]."
- **Motion:** Win: Gear-2 celebration, peak ≤2s, then settle. Miss: soft light-fall, `motion/moment`.
- **Lighting:** Win: the room flashes bright once. Miss: a warm dim, never a blackout.
- **Audio:** Win: triumphant sting; winners' phones Cascade. Miss: warm brass shrug; host pre-fed the consolation line.
- **Timing:** 3s either way, then hand back to the host.
- **Transition:** Machine strikes itself off stage (`ease/exit`); ambient returns.
- **Emotional objective:** Glory or grace — either way, the table got its ninety seconds of fame.

## 7. HARD DECK — "The Crucible"

**Frame 1 — The Wheel**
- **Audience sees:** Stage cuts to near-black. **THE HARD DECK** burns on (Bruno Ace SC). A great dark drum built of every team's crest begins to spin.
- **Motion:** Title stamp; drum spin-up fast, then a long honest deceleration, crests ticking past a fixed pointer.
- **Lighting:** Low and ominous; a single hard pointer-light rakes the passing crests.
- **Audio:** The Hard Deck signature — low strings, pulse. As the drum slows, **every phone in the venue Pulses with each tick.** The stop: one crest ignites; that table's phones strike a Hit.
- **Timing:** 8–12s, host-triggered start.
- **Transition:** The chosen crest sweeps to centre.
- **Emotional objective:** Delicious dread — sixty people feeling the wheel slow in their hands.

**Frame 2 — The Walk-Up**
- **Audience sees:** The chosen crest enthroned centre stage, team name at `display/statement`. The stakes ladder — **5 · 10 · 20 · 40** — builds up the stage's side, bottom rung lit. The base card deals in, huge, face up; the one-time swap sits visibly available.
- **Motion:** Crest sweep (`motion/showpiece`), ladder rungs light bottom-to-top in sequence, card deal-in with weight.
- **Lighting:** A spotlight state: centre bright, surround dark — the theatre's follow-spot, rendered.
- **Audio:** The bed tightens. **[HOST HOLD]** — host invites the team up or to stand; all other phones sit in Eyes Up with the pot mirrored.
- **Timing:** Build 3s; hold at host's pleasure.
- **Emotional objective:** Ceremony. One table has become the show.

**Frame 3 — The Climb**
- **Audience sees:** The team's HIGHER/LOWER choice flashes on stage the instant they commit; a breath of stillness; the next card **flips with a strike**. Right: card ignites, pot counts up, next rung lights. (Wrong: skip to Frame 5's collapse.)
- **Motion:** Choice stamp (`motion/fast`), still beat, 300ms flip, pot roll-up (`ease/land`), rung ignition.
- **Lighting:** Each correct flip raises the stage brightness one step — the light literally climbs the ladder with them.
- **Audio:** Flip-strike felt on **every phone in the room**; rising tension bed one notch tighter per rung.
- **Timing:** Each cycle ~4s plus the team's thinking time; repeats per rung.
- **Transition:** After each correct card — the decision.
- **Emotional objective:** Escalation you can feel in the walls.

**Frame 4 — Stick or Gamble**
- **Audience sees:** The stage transforms: banked pot at `display/statement` centre; flanking it, **STICK** (calm gold-edged) and **GAMBLE** (purple fire, showing the next rung's value). As the team holds their choice on the handset, a charge-bar fills toward it **live on stage** — filling, faltering, filling.
- **Motion:** The mirrored charge is the only movement — honest linear fill, trembling with release/re-press.
- **Lighting:** All light converges on the two monoliths; the filling side glows brighter as it charges.
- **Audio:** Bed drops to a single held pulse. The room chants. The host stokes. **[HOST-PACED — the team owns the trigger.]**
- **Timing:** Unbounded. The hesitation is the content.
- **Transition:** Commit locks: STICK → banking sequence; GAMBLE → one more climb cycle, stakes higher.
- **Emotional objective:** The visible almost — no show has staged a decision being *nearly made* before. This frame is the format's signature image.

**Frame 5 — The Verdict**
- **Audience sees:** **Bank:** the pot detonates upward into the team's score — "Banked. [Team] +40" — Gear-3 celebration. **Bust (wrong/tie):** the ladder's light collapses downward in one dignified fall; the pot zeroes; the stamp: **"Gambled at 40. Respect."**
- **Motion:** Bank: pot numerals fly to the scoreline, light cascade, settle. Bust: falling light, `motion/moment`, then stillness — no shake, no mockery.
- **Lighting:** Bank: room flares gold-less bright (no gold — that's the finale's). Bust: the room dims with the fall, then warms back.
- **Audio:** Bank: triumphant hit; their phones alight. Bust: one falling note, the room's *ooof*, then the respect stamp's soft tick.
- **Timing:** 4s either way; host lands the button line.
- **Transition:** Ladder and cards strike; standings or next round.
- **Emotional objective:** Glory or honoured bravery — next week's teams must *want* to gamble.

## 8. ROUND FINISH — "The Act Break"

**Frame 1 — The Stamp**
- **Audience sees:** **END OF ROUND 2** sweeps across the stage with a single light-pass.
- **Motion:** Stamp + horizontal light sweep, `motion/moment`.
- **Lighting:** One warm flare — a chapter closing, not an alarm.
- **Audio:** Warm stamp-and-sweep sting. Handsets: private round receipts land simultaneously.
- **Timing:** 1.5s.
- **Transition:** The stamp lifts to header position.
- **Emotional objective:** Punctuation. The act is over.

**Frame 2 — Caption One: Speed**
- **Audience sees:** **"Fastest this round: [crest] [TEAM]"** deals in as a broadcast caption.
- **Motion:** Rise-and-settle; crest flares once.
- **Lighting:** Steady; the caption carries its own edge light.
- **Audio:** One tick; that team's phones flare and Cascade.
- **Timing:** 2s.
- **Transition:** Caption slides up a slot.
- **Emotional objective:** An unexpected name-check — speed honoured publicly.

**Frame 3 — Caption Two: The Climber**
- **Audience sees:** **"Biggest climber: [crest] [TEAM] ▲4"** lands beneath the first.
- **Motion:** Same caption language; the ▲ ticks upward once.
- **Lighting:** Unchanged.
- **Audio:** One tick; the climber's phones Cascade.
- **Timing:** 2s.
- **Transition:** Third slot opens.
- **Emotional objective:** The comeback story gets its headline.

**Frame 4 — Caption Three: The Host's Pick**
- **Audience sees:** The host-chosen stat: *"Only one team knew Q7…"* — editable, skippable, theirs.
- **Motion:** Same language, third slot.
- **Lighting:** Unchanged.
- **Audio:** One tick. **[HOST HOLD]** — this caption exists to be talked over.
- **Timing:** Host's.
- **Transition:** Captions fold away together.
- **Emotional objective:** The show hands the host a punchline.

**Frame 5 — The Intermission Canvas**
- **Audience sees:** The stage exhales: ambient bloom breathing, standings resting small to one side, wordmark quiet, and (if set) the venue's framed line — "Kitchen closes at 10" — in the show's own typography.
- **Motion:** Everything settles; the only movement is the 4s bloom breath.
- **Lighting:** Warm, low, steady — the holding shot. The room's own light for the break.
- **Audio:** System audio out entirely. The venue's music or the host's patter owns the air.
- **Timing:** The whole break; the next deal wakes the stage.
- **Transition:** Moment 2, next round.
- **Emotional objective:** Confident rest. A show unafraid of quiet.

## 9. PODIUM — "The Ascent"

**Frame 1 — The Gathering**
- **Audience sees:** The stage empties to the deepest black of the night. One Whisper line, alone: **"The final standings."**
- **Motion:** Slow full fade (the night's only slow transition — earned), then stillness.
- **Lighting:** The room goes properly dark for the first time since the opening. Phones: all Eyes Up, dark, silent.
- **Audio:** Everything out except the heartbeat, slower than ever. The host's voice alone in the dark.
- **Timing:** 4s fade. **[HOST HOLD]**.
- **Transition:** The first pillar stirs.
- **Emotional objective:** Gravity. The night suddenly *matters*.

**Frame 2 — Third Place**
- **Audience sees:** A bronze-edged pillar rises from the stage floor; the third-place crest and name ascend onto it.
- **Motion:** Pillar rise `motion/showpiece` with `ease/settle`; name plate stamps atop.
- **Lighting:** A bronze-warm column of light — the room's first colour since the blackout.
- **Audio:** One rising orchestral note; a single room-wide phone-strike. That team's phones alone break Eyes Up: rank card, warm Hit.
- **Timing:** 3s + applause hold.
- **Transition:** Darkness re-settles; the heartbeat continues.
- **Emotional objective:** Medal-ceremony pride — applause fully released.

**Frame 3 — Second Place**
- **Audience sees:** A taller silver pillar rises… and holds *empty* a beat longer than third's did — every remaining team doing the same maths — then the name lands.
- **Motion:** Rise, deliberate hold, then the plate stamp.
- **Lighting:** Silver-cool column; the extra dark beat before the name is the frame's weapon.
- **Audio:** Rising note, cut before resolution — resolves only when the name lands. The named table's mixed roar (thrilled/gutted) *is* the audio moment.
- **Timing:** 3s rise + the night's longest pre-name pause (host-held).
- **Transition:** The stage holds its breath.
- **Emotional objective:** The maximum-tension frame of the entire night: "if that's not us… we might have won."

**Frame 4 — The Empty Gold Pillar**
- **Audience sees:** The centre pillar rises — taller than both, gold-edged, and **nameless**. An empty throne in a dark room.
- **Motion:** The slowest rise of the sequence; then absolute stillness.
- **Lighting:** The first gold light of the night, thrown upward from an empty platform. Nothing else lit.
- **Audio:** Heartbeat only. **[HOST HOLD]** — the best line of the host's night lives here.
- **Timing:** As long as the host dares.
- **Transition:** The final trigger — into Moment 10.
- **Emotional objective:** The poster image. A whole room staring at an unnamed throne.

**Frame 5 — (The name does not land in this moment)**
- **Audience sees:** The held tableau: bronze named, silver named, gold empty. Phones dark. Room silent or chanting.
- **Motion:** None. This frame is deliberately a freeze.
- **Lighting:** Three columns in the dark; gold the tallest and emptiest.
- **Audio:** Heartbeat. Host voice. Nothing else.
- **Timing:** Continuous with Frame 4 — the storyboard splits it to make one thing explicit: *first place is not a reveal; it is the next moment entire.*
- **Transition:** Detonation → Champion Celebration, Frame 1.
